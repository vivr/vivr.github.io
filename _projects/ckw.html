Scope

CKW is an imports and exports trading company who wanted a redesign of their existing website with the primary objective of promoting the companies services.

Approach

After discussing project deliverables with the client it was decided that the best solution would be to remove some unnecessary content pages and build a one page site showing their main services. Talking extensively with the client allowed me to understand how the website should work and helped me develop a clear vision of how the redesigned site should look and feel.

Production

The redesign is built on HTML and CSS.
